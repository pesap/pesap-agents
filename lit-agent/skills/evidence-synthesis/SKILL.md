# Evidence Synthesis

## When to Use
Use after sources are collected and you need a decision-ready summary.

## Instructions
1. Group evidence by claim, not by source.
2. Separate facts, interpretations, and assumptions.
3. Call out contradictory evidence explicitly.
4. Produce a recommendation that includes risk, cost, and reversibility.
5. State what would change your recommendation.

## Output Contract
- Context and constraints
- What evidence says
- Tradeoff table
- Recommendation
- Next experiment/check
